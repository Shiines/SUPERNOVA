## 1. PARTNERSHIP PROGRAM

### 1.1 Free Partnership Period
From the Effective Date until **June 30, 2027** (the "Free Period"), Studio provides Partner with access to the selected service tier at no monthly charge.

### 1.2 Payment Commencement
Effective **July 1, 2027** ("Launch Date"), Partner's selected monthly fee becomes due automatically. No additional signature or notice is required to trigger payment.

### 1.3 Tier Selection
Partner selects one tier at signing. Tier upgrades are available at any time with 14 days notice.

| Tier | Monthly Fee (AED) | Monthly Fee (USD) | Included per Month |
|------|------------------|-------------------|-------------------|
| **Starter** | AED 12,000 | ~$3,270 | 3 Full Listing Packs + 48h turnaround |
| **Growth** | AED 22,000 | ~$5,990 | 6 Full Listing Packs + 2 Space Previews + 36h turnaround |
| **Scale** | AED 38,000 | ~$10,350 | Unlimited staging + 4 Cinematic Tours/month + 1 Arch Viz/quarter + 24h priority |

## 2. COMMITMENT FEE

### 2.1 Amount
Partner pays a one-time, non-refundable Commitment Fee of **AED 2,500** upon signing.

### 2.2 Application
The Commitment Fee is credited in full against Partner's first monthly invoice on July 1, 2027.

### 2.3 Non-Refundable
The Commitment Fee is non-refundable under any circumstances, including early termination by Partner.

---

## 3. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 3.1 Full Listing Pack (included per tier)
Each Full Listing Pack includes:
- Virtual staging of up to 8 rooms (furnished, styled)
- 1 cinematic property tour (55–70 seconds, 16:9 master)
- 3 social media edits (9:16 Reels format, 45s max)
- Delivery via secure download link

### 3.2 Additional Services
Projects exceeding the monthly tier allocation are available at the following rates:
- Additional Full Listing Pack: AED 8,500
- Space Preview (3 renders): AED 2,500
- Interior Design Brief: AED 4,500
- Architectural Visualization: AED 22,000

### 3.3 Rush Delivery
Same-day delivery (available on Growth and Scale tiers): +AED 1,500 per project.

## 5. PAYMENT TERMS (FROM JULY 1, 2027)

### 5.1 Monthly Invoicing
Studio invoices Partner on the 1st of each month. Payment due within 7 days of invoice date.

### 5.2 Annual Prepay Option
Partner may elect annual prepayment at a **15% discount** on the first renewal (July 1, 2027).
Annual prepay must be elected before June 15, 2027.

| Tier | Annual Prepay (USD) | Saving |
|------|--------------------|----|
| Starter | $33,300 | $5,820 |
| Growth | $61,100 | $10,780 |
| Scale | $105,600 | $18,600 |

### 5.3 Late Payment
Invoices unpaid after 14 days accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Studio may suspend service after 21 days of non-payment without breach of this Agreement.

### 5.4 Payment Methods
Bank transfer (IBAN provided on invoice), Wise, or PayPal Business. Currency: AED or USD at spot rate on invoice date.

### 6.3 Third-Party Tools
Partner acknowledges that Studio uses proprietary rendering and production tools. Partner has no claim over Studio's toolchain or methodology.

## 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

Both parties agree to keep the commercial terms of this Agreement confidential. Partner may reference the partnership publicly ("We work with LIOR Visual Studio") but may not disclose pricing or tier details.

## 8. TERMINATION

### 8.1 By Partner
Partner may terminate this Agreement with **30 days written notice**, effective no earlier than **October 1, 2027** (3 months after Launch Date).

Termination during the Free Period does not entitle Partner to a refund of the Commitment Fee.

### 8.2 By Studio
Studio may terminate with 30 days notice if Partner:
- Fails to pay two consecutive invoices
- Uses Studio's deliverables in a manner that violates applicable law
- Disparages Studio publicly in a materially damaging way

### 8.3 Effect of Termination
Upon termination, all licenses to future deliverables cease. Assets already delivered remain Partner's property.
