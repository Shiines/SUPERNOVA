What you receive
Two directions. Fully annotated.
01
Scale floor plan with furniture placed

A dimensionally accurate layout with every key piece of furniture positioned. Living areas, sleeping zones, circulation paths, storage. You see how the space actually works before committing to anything.

02
Two layout directions

We never deliver one answer. Two distinct approaches: one optimising flow, one maximising usable area. You choose the direction that fits your lifestyle, then we refine from there.

03
Circulation and flow analysis

Every layout comes with a circulation overlay. We annotate the movement paths through the space, identifying bottlenecks, dead zones, and opportunities that flat plans miss entirely.

04
Annotated rationale

Every design decision is explained. Why the bedroom faces this direction, why the kitchen is positioned here, why this circulation path changes how the whole floor feels. Not just a plan. A brief you can hand directly to your contractor.
